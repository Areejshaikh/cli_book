# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Textbook

## Overview

This interactive textbook teaches Physical AI and humanoid robotics in a beginner-friendly way. It combines robotics middleware, simulation, perception, and Vision-Language-Action systems in a single capstone learning experience.

## Curriculum Structure

The textbook is organized into 4 curriculum modules:

1. **The Robotic Nervous System (ROS 2)** - Middleware for robot control
2. **The Digital Twin (Gazebo & Unity)** - Physics simulation and environment building
3. **The AI-Robot Brain (NVIDIA Isaac™)** - Advanced perception, training, and navigation
4. **Vision-Language-Action (VLA)** - Integrating LLMs with Robotics

## Learning Approach

Each chapter follows a consistent structure:
- Short introduction
- Core concept explanation
- Visual or diagram
- 3-bullet summary
- 3-question quiz

The total reading time for the entire book is designed to be ≤ 45 minutes.

## Mobile-First Design

The textbook is optimized for mobile and low-end devices, ensuring accessibility for all learners regardless of their technology resources.
